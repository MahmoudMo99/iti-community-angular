About the Platform

The ITI Community platform is built to simplify training management at ITI Qena branch. It centralizes Rounds, Tracks, Courses, and communication between Students and Instructors.

Core Features

Manage Rounds & Tracks

Organize all Rounds, Tracks, and students in one structured dashboard.

Courses & Materials

Instructors upload and manage lectures, labs, and resources for students.

Student Collaboration

Students can view and connect with peers across different tracks and rounds.
